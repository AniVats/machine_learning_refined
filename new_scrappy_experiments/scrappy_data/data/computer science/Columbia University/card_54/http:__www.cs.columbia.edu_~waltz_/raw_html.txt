Waltz has been Director of the <a href=\"http://www.ccls.columbia.edu\" target=\"_blank\">Center for Computational Learning Systems (CCLS)</a> at <a href=\"http://www.columbia.edu\" target=\"_blank\">Columbia University</a> since 2003. He was formerly President of the <a href=\"http://www.nec-labs.com\" target=\"_blank\">NEC Research Institute</a> in Princeton, and from 1984-1993 was Director of Advanced Information Systems at <a href=\"http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Thinking_Machines\" target=\"_blank\">Thinking Machines Corporation</a> and Professor of <a href=\"http://www.cs.brandeis.edu\" target=\"_blank\">Computer Science at Brandeis University</a>. He had also been Professor of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the <a href=\"http://www.csl.uiuc.edu\" target=\"_blank\">University of Illinois (CSL</a> and<a href=\"http://www.ece.uiuc.edu\" target=\"_blank\"> ECE Department</a>) for 11 years. Waltz served as president of <a href=\"http://www.aaai.org/home.html\" target=\"_blank\">AAAI (American Association for Artificial Intelligence)</a> from 1997-1999, and is a Fellow of AAAI and <a href=\"http://www.acm.org\" target=\"_blank\">ACM (Association for Computing Machinery)</a>, a Senior Member of<a href=\"http://www.ieee.org/portal/site\" target=\"_blank\"> IEEE (Institute for Electrical and Electronics Engineers)</a>, and former Chairman of <a href=\"http://www.sigart.org\" target=\"_blank\">ACM SIGART</a> (Special Interest Group on Artificial Intelligence). He is currently on the <a href=\"http://www.arl.army.mil/www/default.cfm?Action=20\" target=\"_blank\">Army Research Lab</a> Technical Advisory Board and the Advisory Board of the <a href=\"http://www.ihmc.us\" target=\"_blank\">Florida Institute for Human and Machine Cognition</a>, the Technical Advisory Board of <a href=\"http://4c.ucc.ie/web/index.jsp\" target=\"_blank\">4C (Cork Constraint Computation Center, Ireland)</a> and has served on recent external advisory boards for Rutgers University, Carnegie-Mellon University, Brown University, and EPFL (Ecole Polytechnique Federale de Lausanne). He is on the Advisory Board for <a href=\"http://www.computer.org/portal/site/intelligent/\" target=\"_blank\">IEEE Intelligent Systems</a>, and the <a href=\"http://www.cra.org/ccc/\" target=\"_blank\">Computing Community Consortium Boar</a>d of the <a href=\"http://www.cra.org/\">CRA (Computing Research Association),</a> and NSF Computer Science Advisory Board.</p>\n      <p align=\"left\">Dr. Waltz received all his degrees from MIT, including his Ph.D. for work at the<a href=\"http://www.csail.mit.edu/index.php\" target=\"_blank\"> MIT AI Lab</a>. His thesis on computer vision originated the field of <a href=\"http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/History_of_artificial_intelligence#The_golden_years_1956.E2.88.921974\" target=\"_blank\">constraint propagation</a>, and with Craig Stanfill, he originated the field of memory-based reasoning branch of <a href=\"http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Case-based_reasoning\" target=\"_blank\">CBR (Case-Based Reasoning)</a>. His current primary research interest is in machine learning applications, especially to the <a href=\"http://www.encyclopedia.com/doc/1G1-157035557.html\" target=\"_blank\">electric power grid</a>. His research interests have also included massively parallel information retrieval, data mining, learning and automatic classification with applications protein structure prediction, and natural language processing.</p>\n    </div>\n    <!-- InstanceEndEditable -->\n    <div class=\"story\"><!-- InstanceBeginEditable name=\"Additional info\" -->\n    <table width=\"100%\" cellpadding=\"0\" cellspacing=\"0\" summary=\"\">\n      <tbody><tr valign=\"top\">\n        <td class=\"storyLeft\"><p>&nbsp;</p>\n          <p>&nbsp;</p>\n          <h3>&nbsp;</h3>\n          <h3>&nbsp;</h3>\n          </td>\n        </tr>\n    </tbody></table>\n    <!-- InstanceEndEditable --></div>\n  </div>\n  <div id=\"siteInfo\"><a href=\"personal.html\" target=\"_self\">About Me </a>| <a href=\"mailto:waltz@ccls.columbia.edu\" target=\"_blank\">Contact Me </a> | <a href=\"http://www.columbia.edu\" target=\"_blank\">Columbia University</a> | <a href=\"waltzCV.pdf\" target=\"_blank\">CV</a></div>\n</div>\n<!--end pagecell1-->\n<br>\n<script type=\"text/javascript\">\n    <!--\n      var menuitem1 = new menu(7,1,\"hidden\");\n\t\t\tvar menuitem2 = new menu(7,2,\"hidden\");\n\t\t\tvar menuitem3 = new menu(7,3,\"hidden\");\n\t\t\tvar menuitem4 = new menu(7,4,\"hidden\");\n\t\t\tvar menuitem5 = new menu(7,5,\"hidden\");\n\t\t\tvar menuitem6 = new menu(7,6,\"hidden\");\n\t\t\tvar menuitem7 = new menu(7,7,\"hidden\");\n    // -->\n    </script>\n\n<!-- InstanceEnd -->\n</div></body></html>"
