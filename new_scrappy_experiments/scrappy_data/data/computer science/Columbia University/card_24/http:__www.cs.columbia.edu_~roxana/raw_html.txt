</div>\n</div>\n\n    <div class=\"content container\">\n      <p>I am an associate professor of Computer Science at Columbia University. My research spans broad areas of computer systems,\nincluding distributed systems, security and privacy, operating systems, databases, and applications of cryptography and machine\nlearning to systems.\nMuch of my current research aims to expose problems and invent solutions to threats to our data security and privacy in the context of new, emerging technologies, such as cloud computing, mobile devices, and big data.\nI design, build, and evaluate scalable distributed infrastructures that address these problems.\nRead about some of my research <a
