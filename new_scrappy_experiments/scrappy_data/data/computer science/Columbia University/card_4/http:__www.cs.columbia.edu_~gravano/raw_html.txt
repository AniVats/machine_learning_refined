Luis joined Columbia in 1997. At Google, he was a\n      Senior Research Scientist in 2001 (on leave from Columbia) and a\n      Visiting Faculty Researcher in 2018-19 (on sabbatical from\n      Columbia). He received his Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from\n      Stanford University in 1997. He also received an M.S.  degree\n      from Stanford in 1994 and a B.S. degree from the Escuela\n      Superior Latinoamericana de Inform\u00e1tica (ESLAI), Argentina, in\n      1991. His research interests are in databases, information\n      retrieval, and web search. Luis is a recipient of an NSF CAREER\n      award. He has received multiple best paper awards, including at\n      the ACM SIGMOD 2006 and IEEE ICDE 2005 conferences.</p>\n\n    </div>\n    <hr>\n    \n\n    \n</body></html>"
