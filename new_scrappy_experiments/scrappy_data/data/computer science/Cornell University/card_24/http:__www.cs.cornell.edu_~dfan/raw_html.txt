Carpenter Memorial Advising Award (2016),\nthe Douglas Whitney '61 Excellence in Teaching Award (2011), \nthe Zellman Warhaft Commitment to Diversity Award (2008), \nand the Cornell Society of Engineers Achievement Award (2004).  \nFan obtained her Ph.D. in civil and environmental engineering at Cornell University (2002).  \nShe was a research scholar at the International Institute for Applied Systems Analysis (IIASA) \nin Austria (1995).\n</p>\n\n</div>\n\n</div>\n\n<hr>\n\n<div id=\"footer\">\n<!-- The footer-content div contains the Cornell University copyright -->\n<div id=\"footer-content\">\n\t\u00a92020 <a href=\"http://www.cornell.edu/\">Cornell University</a>\n</div>\n</div>\n\n\n\n</div></div></body></html>"
