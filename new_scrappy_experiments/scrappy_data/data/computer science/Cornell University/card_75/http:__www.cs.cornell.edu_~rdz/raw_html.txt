I have worked\n                on a variety of problems in early vision, including\n                motion and stereo; many of these problems can be solved\n                very accurately using algorithms based on graph cuts,\n                which was given the <a href=\"https://www.computer.org/web/tcpami/helmholtz-prize\">Helmholtz\n                Prize</a> at ICCV 2011/13 and the <a href=\"http://eccv2012.unifi.it/conference/awards/\">Koenderink\n                  prize</a> at ECCV 2012 for contribution that have stood the\n                test of time. I served as a Program Chair\n                for <a href=\"http://www.cvpr.org/2007/index.php\">CVPR\n                  2007</a> (the primary North American vision\n                conference), and for the <a href=\"http://www.dsi.unive.it/%7Eiwcv/index.php/pages/home\">International\n                  Workshop on Computer Vision 2008</a>, <a href=\"http://iwcv2010.uniparthenope.it/\">2010</a>, \n                 <a href=\"http://iplab.dmi.unict.it/IWCV/\">2012</a>, <a href=\"http://visionlab.uniss.it/iwcv2014/\">2014</a>, \n\t\t\t\t <a href=\"http://iwcv2016.isasi.cnr.it/index.html\">2016</a> and <a href=\"http://iwcv2018.unimore.it/\">2018</a>\n                (a small workshop for senior vision researchers), and\n                was a General Chair for <a href=\"http://www.pamitc.org/cvpr13/\">CVPR\n                  2013</a>. I\n                was the Editor-in-Chief of the <a href=\"http://computer.org/tpami/\">IEEE Transactions on\n                  Pattern Analysis and Machine Intelligence</a> from\n                2009 through 2012, and from 2013 through mid 2015 I chaired the <a href=\"http://www.computer.org/portal/web/tcpami\">PAMI-TC</a>,\n                which runs the main vision conferences. I also am the\n                president and founder of the <a href=\"http://www.cv-foundation.org/\">Computer Vision\n                  Foundation</a>, a non-profit which now co-sponsors\n                CVPR and ICCV with the IEEE Computer Society, and for which I\n                was awarded the 2016 <a href=\"https://www.computer.org/web/tcpami/mark-everingham-prize\">\nEveringham Prize</a>. In 2018 I was a general chair for the European Conference on Computer Vision (ECCV), and in 2020 I will be a general chair for CVPR.<br>\n              </font></p>\n            <p><font face=\"Century Schoolbook\">Since the fall of 2013 I\n                am at <a href=\"http://tech.cornell.edu/\">CornellNYC\n                  Tech</a> with a joint appointment in Weill Cornell\n                Radiology. </font><font face=\"Century Schoolbook\">My\n                medical work has focused on the problem of MR image\n                reconstruction, as well as on workflow issues.
