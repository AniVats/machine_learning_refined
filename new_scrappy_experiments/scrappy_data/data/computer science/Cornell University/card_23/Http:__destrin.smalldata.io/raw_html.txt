a Professor of Computer Science at Cornell Tech in\n                New York City where she founded the Jacobs Institute's\n                <a href=\"https://tech.cornell.edu/programs/masters-programs/jacobs-technion-cornell-dual-ms-health-tech/\" target=\"_blank\">Health Tech Hub</a>.\n                She holds The Robert V. Tishman Founder's Chair and serves\n                as the Associate Dean for Impact. Her research interests are at the\n                intersection of user-centric data applications, personalization,\n                and privacy\n                <a href=\"https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lAEhSGYEHWU\">(TEDMED)</a>.\n                Estrin co-founded the non-profit startup,\n                <a href=\"http://www.openmhealth.org/\">Open mHealth</a>\n                and has served on several scientific advisory boards\n                for early stage mobile health startups. She is currently serving as\n                a part-time Amazon Scholar.\n            </p>\n\n            <p> Before joining Cornell University Estrin was the Founding Director of\n                the NSF Center for Embedded Networked Sensing (CENS) at UCLA; pioneering\n                the development of mobile and wireless systems to collect and analyze real\n                time data about the physical world. Her honors include: ACM Athena Lecture (2006),\n                Anita Borg Institute's Women of Vision Award for Innovation (2007),\n                The American Academy of Arts and Sciences (2007), The National Academy of\n                Engineering (2009), The IEEE Internet Award (2017), MacArthur Fellow (2018),\n                and The National Academy of Medicine (2019).\n            </p>\n\n            <p>Current research collaborators include:\n                <a href=\"http://infosci.cornell.edu/faculty/tanzeem-choudhury\" target=\"_blank\">Tanzeem Choudhury</a>,\n                <a href=\"http://www.nyu.edu/projects/nissenbaum/\" target=\"_blank\">Helen Nissenbaum</a>,\n                <a href=\"https://www.vassar.edu/faculty/jawaterman/\" target=\"_blank\">Jason Waterman</a>,\n                <a href=\"http://tech.cornell.edu/people/jp-pollak\" target=\"_blank\">JP Pollak</a>,\n                <a href=\"https://people.jacobs.cornell.edu/mor/\" target=\"_blank\">Mor Naaman</a>,\n                <a href=\"http://www.cs.cornell.edu/~jnfoster/\" target=\"_blank\">Nate Foster</a>,\n                <a href=\"http://www.nixdell.com/\" target=\"_blank\">Nicki Dell</a>.\n            </p>\n\n            <div id=\"projects_area\">\n                <div id=\"projects\">\n                    <h1>Projects</h1>\n                    <ul>\n                        <li><a href=\"http://smalldata.io/\" target=\"_blank\">Small Data</a>\n                            <p>Small Data are the myriad of data traces we each generate everyday.\n                                Unfortunately, that data is often unavailable to us in a form that we\n                                can make sense of or act upon. Imagine a special kind of app running\n                                in the cloud that privately and securely turns your small data into big insights.</p>\n                            <p><strong>\n                                </strong></p><center><strong>Creating an ecosystem around small data</strong></center><strong>\n                            </strong><p></p>\n                            <center><a href=\"http://smalldata.io/\" target=\"_blank\"><img id=\"small_data_image\" class=\"img-responsive\" src=\"images/small_data_image.png\"></a></center>\n                        </li>\n\n                        <li><a href=\"http://smalldata.io/\" target=\"_blank\">Immersive Recommendations</a>\n                            <p>We explore a personalization approach in which individuals\n                                actively harness their own digital traces. We focus in\n                                particular on algorithms, systems, and rich user interactions\n                                that make personalization both immersive--through the use\n                                of diverse data sources--and intentional--through transparency\n                                and user-modeling inputs that allow the end user to amplify\n                                their aspirational-behaviors relative to historically-observed behaviors.</p>\n                            <center><img id=\"immersive_rec\" class=\"img-responsive\" src=\"images/immersive_rec.png\"></center>\n                        </li>\n                        <!--<p><strong><u>Spring 2018 Teaching:</u></strong></p>-->\n                        <!--<p><strong><a href=\"https://info5555.github.io/info5555-CT-SP18.github.io/\" target=\"_blank\">INFO5555 Health Tech, Data, Systems</a></strong></p>-->\n                        <!--<p><strong><a href=\"https://pages.github.coecis.cornell.edu/cs5450/website/\" target=\"_blank\">CS5450 Networks and Distributed Computing,</a> with  <a href=\"https://www.cs.cornell.edu/~shmat/\" target=\"_blank\">Vitaly Shmatikov</a></strong></p>-->\n\n                        <li>\n                            <a href=\"https://tech.cornell.edu/programs/masters-programs/jacobs-technion-cornell-dual-ms-health-tech/\" target=\"_blank\">Health Tech Hub</a>\n\n                            <p>The Masters of Science program in Health Tech was launched\n                                in 2015 and we are accepting applications for Fall 2020.</p>\n                            <center><a href=\"https://tech.cornell.edu/jacobs-technion-cornell-institute/\" target=\"_blank\">\n                                <img src=\"images/Horizontal Cornell + Jacobs - Black.png\" id=\"healthier_life_hub_image\" class=\"img_responsive\" style=\"height: 80px; The open API platform gives developers a way to\n                                easily produce, access and integrate clinically meaningful and\n                                actionable data. Open mHealth builds products designed to support\n                                collaborative, connected, personalized care, that demonstrate\n                                the power of our open platform.</p>\n\n                        </li>\n                    </ul>\n                </div>\n            </div>\n        </div>\n    </div>\n    <div id=\"media_area\" class=\"row\">\n        <div id=\"press\" class=\"col-md-6\">\n            <h1>Funders and Collaborators</h1>\n\n            <!--<h4> Current</h4>-->\n            <ul>\n                <li>\n                    <a href=\"http://www.nsf.gov/\" target=\"_blank\">National Science Foundation</a>\n                    (Award #1343058, 1642120, 1700832)\n                </li>\n                <li>\n                    <a href=\"http://www.nih.gov/\" target=\"_blank\">National Institutes of Health;
