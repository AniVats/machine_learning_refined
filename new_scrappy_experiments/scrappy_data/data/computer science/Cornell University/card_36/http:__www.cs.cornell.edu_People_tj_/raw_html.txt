He has served a two-year term as Chair of the Department of Information Science. He joined Cornell in 2001 after finishing his Ph. D. as a student of Prof. <a href=\"http://www-ai.cs.uni-dortmund.de/PERSONAL/morik.html\">Morik</a> at the <a href=\"http://www-ai.cs.uni-dortmund.de/\">AI-unit</a> of the <a href=\"http://www.uni-dortmund.de/\">University of Dortmund</a>, from where he also received a Diplom in Computer Science in 1997. Between 2000 and 2001 he worked as a PostDoc at the GMD in the Knowledge Discovery Team of the <a href=\"http://www.iais.fraunhofer.de/\">Institute for Autonomous Intelligent Systems</a>. From 1994 to 1996 he spent one and a half years at <a href=\"http://www.cs.cmu.edu/\">Carnegie Mellon University</a> as a visiting scholar of <a href=\"http://www.cs.cmu.edu/~tom\">Prof. Tom Mitchell</a>. Working with his students and collaborators, his papers won 8 Best Paper Awards and 4 Test-of-Time Awards. Thorsten Joachims is an ACM Fellow, AAAI Fellow, and Humboldt Fellow. \n[<a href=\"publications/joachims_cv.pdf\">Curriculum Vita</a>]</p>\n\n  </td>\n\n </tr>\n\n\n <tr>\n\n  <td width=\"10\"
