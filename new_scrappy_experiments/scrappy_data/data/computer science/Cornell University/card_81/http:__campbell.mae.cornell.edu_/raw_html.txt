autonomous and semi-autonomous systems, with a special emphasis on robotics and aerospace applications. Specific research areas include sensor fusion and probabilistic perception, control and planning in the presence of uncertainties, human decision modeling, and human-robot interaction.</p>\n<p>I co-direct the <a href=\"http://cornell-asl.org/wiki\">Autonomous Systems Lab</a> with <a href=\"http://verifiablerobotics.com/\">Prof. Hadas Kress Gazit</a>. &nbsp;The <a href=\"http://cornell-asl.org/wiki\">ASL wiki</a> includes most of the hardware and undergraduate/Masters of Engineering projects, whereas this wiki focuses on the research of my group.</p>
