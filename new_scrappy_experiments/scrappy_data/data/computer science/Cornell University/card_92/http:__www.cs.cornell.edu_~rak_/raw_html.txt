Knepper\">\n        </figure>\n      </div>\n      <div>\n        <h2>Research Interests</h2>\n        <p>\nMany important robotics problems have analogues in human experience. Humans are uniquely capable of establishing ad-hoc teaming relationships, adapting to evolving circumstances, solving complex tasks, manipulating objects with dexterity, and acting competently under uncertainty. My research explores algorithmic techniques that imbue robots with these same competencies. Leveraging insights from psychology, sociology, and linguistics, I make robots that interact with humans through natural communication modalities such as natural language and gesture, so that they can be programmed without special training and can operate as peers with humans in the workplace and the home. Robots must be aware of their environment and responsive to changes within it. I often employ Ikea furniture assembly in the laboratory as a challenge problem to demonstrate techniques in planning, control, perception and human interaction.\n        </p>\n        <h2>Teaching</h2>\n        <ul>\n          <li>\n            CS 4750 / CS 5750 / ECE 4770 / MAE 4760:\n            <a href=\"http://www.cs.cornell.edu/courses/cs4750/\" target=\"_blank\">Foundations of Robotics</a>\n            (Fall 2019, Fall 2018, Fall 2017, Fall 2016)\n          </li>\n          <li>\n            CS 4752 / CS 5752 / MAE 4750 / MAE 5750:\n            <a href=\"http://www.cs.cornell.edu/courses/cs4752/\" target=\"_blank\">Robotic Manipulation</a>\n            (Fall 2015, Spring 2015)\n          </li>\n          <li>\n            CS 4754 / INFO 4410:\n            <a href=\"https://classes.cornell.edu/browse/roster/SP15/class/CS/4754\" target=\"_blank\">Human Robot Interaction</a>\n            (Spring 2015)\n          </li>\n          <li>\n            CS 6751 / MAE 6730:\n            <a href=\"http://www.cs.cornell.edu/courses/cs6751/\" target=\"_blank\">Introduction to Robotic Mobile Manipulation</a>\n            (Spring 2018, Spring 2017, Spring 2016, Fall 2014)\n          </li>\n        </ul>\n        <h2>Sponsors</h2>\n        <p>I gratefully acknowledge my research sponsors:</p>\n        <div>\n          <img src=\"img/onr.png\" alt=\"Office of Naval Research>\">\n          <img src=\"img/nsf.png\" alt=\"National Science Foundation\">\n          <img src=\"img/afosr.png\" alt=\"Air Force Office of Scientific Research\">\n          <img src=\"img/amazon.png\" alt=\"Amazon\">\n        </div>\n        <p>and education sponsors:</p>\n        <div>\n          <img src=\"img/intel.png\" alt=\"Intel\">\n        </div>\n      </div>\n    </main>\n  \n\n</body></html>"
