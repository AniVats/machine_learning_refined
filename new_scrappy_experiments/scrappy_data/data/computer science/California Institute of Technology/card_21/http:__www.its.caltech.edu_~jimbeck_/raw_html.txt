Although I no longer run a research group, I am\ncollaborating with other researchers at different institutions. I have also been pursuing research\ninterests on my own, especially on the foundations of quantum mechanics.<br><br>\nMy research is focused primarily on stochastic dynamics where I use Bayesian probability as a\nlogic to handle the uncertainties involved in the modeling of physical systems and their\nexcitation. I have a particular interest in the dynamics of structural systems, including stochastic\nresponse prediction under earthquakes, and using sensor data from monitoring structures for\nsystem identification and structural health monitoring.
