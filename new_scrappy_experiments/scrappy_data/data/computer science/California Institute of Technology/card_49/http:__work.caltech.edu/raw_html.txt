Abu-Mostafa is a Professor of <a href=\"http://www.ee.caltech.edu/\">Electrical Engineering</a> and <a href=\"http://www.cms.caltech.edu/\">Computer Science</a> at the California Institute of Technology, and Chairman of <i>Machine Learning Consultants LLC</i>. His main fields of expertise are machine learning and computational finance. He is the author of Amazon's machine learning bestseller <a href=\"http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/1600490069\">Learning from Data</a>. His <a href=\"telecourse.html\">MOOC</a> on machine learning has attracted more than two million views.</p>\n\n  <p>Dr. Abu-Mostafa received the Clauser Prize for the most original doctoral thesis at Caltech. He received the ASCIT Teaching Awards in 1986, 1989 and 1991, the GSC Teaching Awards in 1995 and 2002, and the <a href=\"http://www.caltech.edu/article/11719\">Richard P. Feynman prize for excellence in teaching</a> in 1996. He was the founding Program Chairman of the annual conference on Neural Information Processing Systems (NIPS), and a founding member of the IEEE Neural Networks Council. He chaired the second and fourth international conferences on Neural Networks in the Capital Markets (NNCM-94 and NNCM-96), and the sixth international conference on Computational Finance (CF-99). In 2005, the Hertz Foundation established a perpetual graduate fellowship named <b><i>the Abu-Mostafa Fellowship</i></b> in his honor.</p>\n\n<p>Dr. Abu-Mostafa currently serves on a number of scientific advisory boards, and has served as a technical consultant on machine learning for several companies, including Citibank for 9 years.
