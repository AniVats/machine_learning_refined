   <div id=\"content\">\n                    <h2>About Me</h2>\n                    <p><br>I am a Rosenberg Scholar and <a href=\"http://cms.caltech.edu/people/klbouman\">Assistant Professor </a> of Computing and Mathematical Sciences (CMS) and Electrical Engineering at Caltech in Pasadena, California. \n                    My research focuses on computational imaging: designing systems that tightly integrate algorithm and sensor design, making it possible to observe phenomena previously difficult or impossible to measure with traditional approaches. \n                    My group at Caltech combines ideas from signal processing, computer vision, machine learning, and physics to find and exploit hidden signals for both scientific discovery and technological innovation. \n
