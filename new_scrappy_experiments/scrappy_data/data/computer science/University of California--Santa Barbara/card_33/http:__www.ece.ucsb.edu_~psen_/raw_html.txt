He then attended\n<a href=\"http://www.stanford.edu/\" target=\"_blank\">Stanford University</a> where he received his M.S. in <a href=\"http://ee.stanford.edu/\" target=\"_blank\">Electrical Engineering</a> in 1998 in the area of electron-beam lithography.  In 2000, he joined the <a href=\"http://graphics.stanford.edu/\" target=\"_blank\">Stanford Graphics Lab</a> where he did research on real-time rendering and computational photography.\nHe received his Ph.D. in\n<a href=\"http://ee.stanford.edu/\" target=\"_blank\">Electrical Engineering</a> in June 2006, advised by <a href=\"http://graphics.stanford.edu/~hanrahan/\" target=\"_blank\">Dr. Pat Hanrahan</a>. \nHis research interests \ninclude algorithms for image synthesis, computational image processing, and computational photography, and he\nis a co-author of over 50 technical publications, including more than ten SIGGRAPH/SIGGRAPH Asia/ToG publications. I study\nnovel rendering algorithms to accelerate global illumination, as well as image-based techniques\nfor capturing light transport.  I am also interested in real-time rendering and graphics hardware.  On the computational image processing side, I am interested in novel imaging architectures as well as new ways to combine processing and acquisition to capture new kinds of imagery.\n</p>  \n\n\n\n\n<h1>Current Students</h1>\n<p>\n\nAt UCSB, I have the pleasure of working with many great graduate students:\n</p><ul> \n<li><a href=\"https://www.ece.ucsb.edu/~sbako/\" target=\"_blank\">Steve Bako</a> -- B.S., University of California, Berkeley</li> \n<li><a href=\"https://www.ece.ucsb.edu/~abadki/\" target=\"_blank\">Abhishek Badki</a> -- B.S., National Institute of Technology (NIT) India</li> \n<li><a href=\"https://prashnani.github.io/\" target=\"_blank\">Ekta Prashnani</a> -- B.S., Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Gandhinagar, India.</li>\n<li>Qiaodong Cui -- B.S., Beihang University, China </li>\n<li><a href=\"https://www.ehsansayyad.com/\" target=\"_blank\">Ehsan Sayyad</a> -- B.S., Sharif University, M.F.A, Sharif University of Tehran, Iran</li>\n<li>Trevor Morris -- B.S., University of California, Santa Barbara</li>\n<li>Shayan Sadigh -- B.S., University of California, Santa Barbara</li>\n<li>Adam Schmidt -- B.S., University of California, Santa Barbara</li>\n</ul>\n\n\nI am also looking for a several more Ph.D. students who are interested in computer\ngraphics, computer vision and image processing.
