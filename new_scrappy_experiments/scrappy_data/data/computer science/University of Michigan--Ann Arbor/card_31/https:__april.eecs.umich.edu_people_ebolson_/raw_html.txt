He is the director of the\n<a href=\"http://april.eecs.umich.edu\">APRIL robotics lab</a>, which\nstudies Autonomy, Perception, Robotics, Interfaces, and Learning. He\nis also CEO of <a href=\"http://maymobility.com\">May Mobility,\nInc.</a>, which develops driverless shuttles.</p>\n\n<p>In 2010, he led the winning team in the <a href=\"http://april.eecs.umich.edu/magic\">MAGIC 2010 competition</a> by\ndeveloping a team of 14 robots that semi-autonomously explored and\nmapped a large-scale urban environment. For winning, the\nU.S. Department of Defense awarded him $750,000. He was named one of\nPopular Science's \"Brilliant Ten\" in September, 2012. A few of his\nawards include the DARPA Young Faculty Award (2013) and the College of\nEngineering Education Excellence Education Award (2015).\n\n</p><p>He received a PhD from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology in\n2008 for his work in robust robot mapping. During his time as a PhD\nstudent, he was a core member of their <a href=\"http://grandchallenge.mit.edu\">DARPA Urban Challenge Team</a>\nwhich finished the race in 4th place. His work on autonomous cars\ncontinued in cooperation with Ford Motor Company on the <a href=\"http://april.eecs.umich.edu/ngv\">Next Generation Vehicle</a>\nproject. In 2016, he joined the <a href=\"http://tri.global\">Toyota\nResearch Institute</a> where he was Co-Director for Autonomous Driving\nDevelopment. In 2017, he left TRI to found a new company, <a href=\"https://maymobility.com/\">May Mobility\nInc.</a>, developing autonomous vehicles. He maintains an active research\nprogram at the University of Michigan.</p>\n\n<p>He is well-known in the robotics community for his work in\nperception algorithms, mapping, and planning. He was one of the\noriginal creators of the middleware package <a href=\"http://lcm-proj.github.io\">LCM</a>, and the creator of <a href=\"http://april.eecs.umich.edu/apriltag\">AprilTags</a>, a popular\nvisual fiducial tool.\n\n</p></div>\n\n<h2>Selected Talks (General Audience)</h2>\n\n<hr>\n\n<!--\n<P>I was interviewed on the <a href=\"http://www.newstalkzb.co.nz/on-air/wellington/tim-fookes-morning-show/\">Tim Fookes Morning Show</a> in New Zealand.</P>\n\n<a href=\"/media/people/ebolson/tim-fookes-interview.mp3\">Tim Fookes Morning Show Interview</a> (March 17, 2015)<br>\n\n<hr>\n\n<P>I was interviewed by Audrow Nash for RoboticsPodcast.com.</P>\n\n<a href=roboticspodcast.com>roboticspodcast.com</a>.
