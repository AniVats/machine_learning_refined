My work is at the boundary of HCI and Data Mining. Specific research projects range from modeling behaviors at a very large-scale (search, social networks evolution, learning, meme and rumor spread, programming, text production and consumption, etc.) and applying those models to constructing better HCI systems.  This is primarily at Internet scales and it gives me an excuse to work on lots of different things I like... data/text mining, visualizations, social network analysis, etc.\n\n</p><p>Before <a href=\"http://www.cs.washington.edu/\">grad school</a> I was a researcher at <a href=\"http://www.hpl.hp.com/research/idl/\" class=\"udrline\">HP Labs' Information Dynamics Group</a> and at <a href=\"http://www.parc.com\">Xerox PARC</a>.
