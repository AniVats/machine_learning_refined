                            <h1 class=\"title\" id=\"page-title\">4+1 MS with Concentration in Digital Arts</h1>\n                                                \n\t\t\t\t\t<div class=\"tabs\"></div>       \t \t\t\t        \t\t\t        \t\t\t\n        \t\t\t    <div id=\"node-1141\" class=\"node node-page clearfix row dept-landing-container\" about=\"/graduate/ms-programs/41-ms-concentration-digital-arts\" typeof=\"foaf:Document\">\n\n  \n  \n  <div class=\"content\">\n            <section class=\"intro\">\n      <div class=\"field field-name-field-intro field-type-text-long field-label-hidden\"><div class=\"field-items\"><div class=\"field-item even\"><p>Want to take your computer science and digital arts skills and research farther? Consider the 4 + 1 MS degree program with a Concentration in Digital Arts. <strong>The deadline for Fall 2020&nbsp;admission is December 15, 2019</strong>. <strong>Apply online <a href=\"https://app.applyyourself.com/AYApplicantLogin/fl_ApplicantLogin.asp?id=dart-grad\">here</a>.</strong></p></div></div></div>    </section>\n        \n        \n    <div class=\"field-collection-container clearfix\">\n\n<div class=\"field field-name-field-section field-type-field-collection field-label-hidden\">\n    <div class=\"field-items\">\n                    \n              <section class=\"dept-section field-item even\"><div class=\"field-collection-view clearfix view-mode-full\"><div class=\"entity entity-field-collection-item field-collection-item-field-section clearfix\" about=\"/field-collection/field-section/1756\" typeof=\"\">\n  <div class=\"content\">\n    \n\t<h3 class=\"field-item even\">Eligability</h3>\n<div class=\"field field-name-field-section-body field-type-text-long field-label-hidden\"><div class=\"field-items\"><div class=\"field-item even\"><p>Current Dartmouth students who are doing the minor in digital arts and have completed CS1 and CS10, can earn an MS degree in four terms, or 12 credits. Six of the credits are courses in computer science and art, the other six are independent research (<a href=\"http://dartmouth.smartcatalogiq.com/en/current/orc/Departments-Programs-Graduate/Computer-Science\">see details in ORC</a>). Students are encouraged to apply in the spring of their junior year. Students are also encouraged to work on projects in the DALI lab during the year, to augment your skill set (<a href=\"http://DALI.dartmouth.edu\">DALI.dartmouth.edu</a>). If you are interested, please contact <a href=\"https://web.cs.dartmouth.edu/people/lorie-loeb\">Lorie Loeb</a>. Juniors and seniors can apply using the current graduate application form. No GRE required and no charge to apply. Full scholarships are available to qualifying students.</p>\n</div></div></div>  </div>\n</div>\n</div><span class=\"marker\"></span></section>\n                      \n              <section class=\"dept-section field-item odd\"><div class=\"field-collection-view clearfix view-mode-full\"><div class=\"entity entity-field-collection-item field-collection-item-field-section clearfix\" about=\"/field-collection/field-section/1526\" typeof=\"\">\n  <div class=\"content\">\n    \n\t<h3 class=\"field-item even\">Scholarships and Costs</h3>\n<div class=\"field field-name-field-section-body field-type-text-long field-label-hidden\"><div class=\"field-items\"><div class=\"field-item even\"><p>Dartmouth College tuition for 2019-2020 will be set in early 2019 (tuition for 2017-2018 was $17,156 per term). There is a 50% tuition scholarship for students accepted to the 4 + 1 program. Dartmouth students with a B+ or better average in their undergraduate major and their CS courses will be considered for 100% tuition scholarships.&nbsp;Students also pay for the college health insurance plan, unless they have comparable insurance already. The insurance fee for 2019-2020 will be set in early 2019&nbsp;(detailed information on current fees is available <a href=\"https://www.dartmouth.edu/~control/docs/studentfin/charges1718.pdf\">here</a>). Students are responsible for covering their living expenses.</p>\n<p>Note: Once admitted, international students must provide written certification of financial support for the MS program.</p>\n</div></div></div>  </div>\n</div>\n</div><span class=\"marker\"></span></section>\n                      \n              <section class=\"dept-section field-item even\"><div class=\"field-collection-view clearfix view-mode-full\"><div class=\"entity entity-field-collection-item field-collection-item-field-section clearfix\" about=\"/field-collection/field-section/1746\" typeof=\"\">\n  <div class=\"content\">\n    \n\t<h3 class=\"field-item even\">Admission Requirements</h3>\n<div class=\"field field-name-field-section-body field-type-text-long field-label-hidden\"><div class=\"field-items\"><div class=\"field-item even\"><p>The required components of your application are:</p>\n<ul><li>The <a href=\"https://app.applyyourself.com/AYApplicantLogin/fl_ApplicantLogin.asp?id=dart-grad\">online application form</a></li>\n<li>Three (3) letters of recommendation</li>\n<li>A 100-line sample of code you have written in any language. Explain in one paragraph why you chose this code. Please do not include more than 100 lines.</li>\n<li>Submit 3-5 samples of your creative work. These could be photos of your work, videos, or sound files. Include a link to your online portfolio, if you have one. Please specify what your contribution to the work is and why you chose it as an example.</li>\n<li>All applicants should upload a scanned copy of their transcripts. <strong>Applicants should not send an official transcript to the Graduate Office unless they are admitted and accept our offer of Admission</strong>.</li>\n<li>These items must reach us by <strong>December 15, 2019</strong>&nbsp;in order for your application to receive full consideration.</li>\n</ul></div></div></div>  </div>\n</div>\n</div><span class=\"marker\"></span></section>\n                      \n              <section class=\"dept-section field-item odd\"><div class=\"field-collection-view clearfix view-mode-full field-collection-view-final\"><div class=\"entity entity-field-collection-item field-collection-item-field-section clearfix\" about=\"/field-collection/field-section/1751\" typeof=\"\">\n  <div class=\"content\">\n    \n\t<h3 class=\"field-item even\">OPTIONAL Application Materials</h3>\n<div class=\"field field-name-field-section-body field-type-text-long field-label-hidden\"><div class=\"field-items\"><div class=\"field-item even\"><p>If you have anything else you think would help us get a feel for your work and your interests, please upload them.
