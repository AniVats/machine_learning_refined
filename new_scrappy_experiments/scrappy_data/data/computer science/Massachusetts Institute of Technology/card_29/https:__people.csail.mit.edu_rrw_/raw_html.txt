Interests</font></h3>\n    In general, I try to understand what is easy and what is hard to compute (independently of any particular computer). I work in algorithm design and complexity theory, and I especially like connections between the two subjects. I think about many questions, but a few of them haunt me more than others. Some examples: <i>Can the existence of an algorithm for a problem be used to prove that other algorithms <b>cannot</b> exist for other problems?
