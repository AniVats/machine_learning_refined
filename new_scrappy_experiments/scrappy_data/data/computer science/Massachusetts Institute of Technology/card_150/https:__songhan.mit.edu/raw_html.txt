professor at MIT EECS. Dr. Han received the Ph.D. degree in Electrical Engineering from Stanford advised by Prof. Bill Dally. Dr. Han\u2019s research focuses on efficient deep learning computing. He proposed \u201cDeep Compression\u201d that can compress neural networks by an order of magnitude, and the hardware implementation \u201cEfficient Inference Engine\u201d that brings compressed weights and <span class=\"has-inline-color has-black-color\">sparsity</span> into deep learning accelerators.
