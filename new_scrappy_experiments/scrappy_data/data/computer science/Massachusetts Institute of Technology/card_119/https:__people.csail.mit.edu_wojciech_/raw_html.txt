In 2009, he received the Significant New Researcher Award from ACM Siggraph. In 2012, Matusik received the DARPA Young Faculty Award and he was named a Sloan Research Fellow.\nIn 2014, he received Ruth and Joel Spira Award for Excellence in Teaching.\n<hr>\n\n\n\n<h2>\nResearch Interests:\n</h2>\n<h3>\nComputer Graphics: data-driven methods, physics-based simulation, appearance modeling, computational displays\n<br><br>\nComputional Design and Fabrication: additive manufacturing, textile manufacturing/functional fibers, design/simulation tools, inverse problems, topology optimization\n<br><br>\nComputer Vision: inverse problems, data-driven methods, gaze models, computational photography, multi-modal learning \n<br><br>\nRobotics: computational design/simulation for robotics, soft robotics, tactile sensing/modeling, UAVs \n<br><br>\nHCI: design tools for fabrication, crowdsourcing \n<br>\n</h3>\n\n<hr>\n<h2>\nResearch Highlights: \n</h2>\n<h3>\n<a href=\"http://news.mit.edu/search?keyword=matusik\">MIT News</a>\n<br><br>\n<a href=\"https://www.csail.mit.edu/search?keywords=Matusik&amp;f%5B0%5D=page_type%3Anews\">CSAIL</a>\n<br>\n</h3>\n\n<hr>\n<h2>\nJoining the Group: \n</h2>\n<h3>\nWe are always looking for talented researchers to join our group.\n<br> <br>\nIf you would like to apply for a postdoctoral position then send me an email. \n<br>\nIf you would like to work with me during your PhD, apply directly to the MIT EECS PhD program and indicate my name as a potential advisor. \n<br>\nIf you are an MIT undergraduate, send me (or other group members) a short note. \n<br>\n\n</h3>\n\n\n\n<hr>\n<h2>\n<a href=\"http://cfg.mit.edu/publications\">Publications</a> (also <a href=\"https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=wbIMbL8AAAAJ&amp;hl=en&amp;oi=ao\">Google Scholar)</a>\n</h2>\n\n<hr>\n\n<h2>\n<a href=\"http://cfg.mit.edu/team\">Current Group Members and Alumni</a> <br>\n</h2>\n\n<hr>\n\n<h2>\nTeaching\n</h2>\n\n<h3>\n6.01: Introduction to EECS I -
