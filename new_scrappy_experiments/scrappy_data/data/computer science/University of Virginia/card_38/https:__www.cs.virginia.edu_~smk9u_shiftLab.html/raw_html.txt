With the rising volumes of data, our systems must be able to store, analyze, and transform massive amount of data for useful computation. Unfortunately, computation nowadays is bottlenecked by data storage and movement. The mission of the ShiftLab  is to design new computing systems with a balanced focus on memory and compute for future data intensive applications. \n\t\t\t\t\t\n\t\t\t\t\t\n\t\t\t\t  </div>
