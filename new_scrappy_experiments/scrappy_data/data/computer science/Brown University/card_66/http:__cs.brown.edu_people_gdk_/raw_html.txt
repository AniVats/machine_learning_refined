name=\"Research\"></a><h1>Research</h1>\n\n<p>\nMy research aims to\nbuild intelligent, autonomous, general-purpose robots \nthat are generally capable in a wide variety of tasks\nand environments. \n</p>\n\n<p>\nI focus on understanding how to\ndesign agents that learn \nabstraction hierarchies that enable fast, goal-oriented planning.\nI develop and\napply techniques from machine learning, reinforcement learning, optimal control and planning\nto construct well-grounded hierarchies that result in fast planning for common cases,\nand\nare robust to uncertainty at\nevery level of control.\n</p>\n\n<p>\nI believe that it will take advances in all of these areas, and additionally advances in how to <i>integrate</i> these areas,\nto solve the AI problem.\n</p>\n\n<p>\nThis video of some of my recent work is a good indicator of my interests - it combines ideas from\nhierarchical reinforcement learning, probabilistic machine learning, task-level planning, and robotics\nto create a robot that autonomously learns an abstract symbolic model of an environment and then uses it to plan:\n</p><center>\n<iframe width=\"560\" height=\"315\"
