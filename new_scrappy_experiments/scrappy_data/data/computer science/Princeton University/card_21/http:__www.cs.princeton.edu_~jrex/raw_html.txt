Jennifer is co-author of the book \n<a href=\"https://www.pearson.ch/Informatik/InternetWeb-Design/Grundlagen/EAN/9780201710885/Web-Protocols-and-Practice\">Web\nProtocols and Practice: HTTP/1.1, Networking Protocols, Caching, and\nTraffic Measurement</a> (Addison-Wesley, May 2001) and co-editor of \n<a href=\"pu-eng-book.pdf\">She's an Engineer? Jennifer served as the\nchair of <a href=\"https://www.acm.org/sigcomm/\">ACM SIGCOMM</a> from\n2003 to 2007, and has served on the ACM Council, the board\nof the Computing Research Association, the advisory council\nof the Computer and Information Science and Engineering directorate\nat NSF, and the Computing Community Consortium.  She received her BSE degree \nin electrical engineering from Princeton University in 1991, and her \nMSE and PhD degrees in computer science and electrical engineering \nfrom the University of
