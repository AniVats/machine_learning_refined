clearfix\">\nRamesh has been an active researcher in Computer Vision, Artificial Intelligence, Multimedia Computing, Experiential computing, and Digital Health. While at the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, he founded and directed artificial intelligence laboratory in 1987; and at University of California, San Diego, he founded visual computing lab in 1995.\u00a0 At University of California, Irvine, He is founding director of <a href=\"http://futurehealth.ics.uci.edu/\">UCI Institute for Future Health</a>. He was also the founding Editor-in-Chief of IEEE MultiMedia magazine and Machine Vision and Applications journal.\u00a0 His co-authored and co-edited books include two text books: Machine Vision (published in 1995), and Multimedia Computing (published in 2014). Ramesh has been elected Fellow of ACM, IEEE, AAAS, IAPR, AAAI, and SPIE. He is the recipient of several awards including the ACM SIGMM Technical Achievement Award 2010. His current research is in building computing and data-driven <strong><em>Navigational</em></strong> approaches for<strong>\u00a0Future Health</strong>.\u00a0 He is Chief Technical Advisor for building Open Source Personal Health Navigator Project at Peng Cheng Laboratory in Shenzhen.\nRamesh co-founded multiple companies, managed them in initial stages, and then turned them over to professional management.\u00a0 He enjoys working with companies, is involved in research, and enjoys writing.\nRecently IEEE TCMC announced him to be the winner of Impact award for his \u201cpioneering and wide-spread impact to multimedia computing for the past four decades.\u201d\n<em><span style=\"color: #ff6600;\"><strong>Coming Events:</strong></span></em>\n<ol>\nKeynote Talk at <a href=\"https://meeting.nutrition.org/\">Nutrition 2020</a>: May 30-June 2, 2020, Seattle, WA USA.\nKeynote talk at<a href=\"http://icmr2020.org/\"> ACM ICMR</a>: What Should I do?, June 9-11, 2020, Dublin, Ireland.\n</ol>\n<em><span style=\"color: #ff6600;\"><strong>Vision, Passion, and Current Research is described in</strong>: </span></em><a href=\"https://www.dropbox.com/s/6p5pwcghmc4wbyy/Personal%20Health%20Navigator%20-%20Computer%20April%202019.pdf?dl=0\">A Navigational Approach to Health</a>;\u00a0 in IEEE Computer Magazine.
