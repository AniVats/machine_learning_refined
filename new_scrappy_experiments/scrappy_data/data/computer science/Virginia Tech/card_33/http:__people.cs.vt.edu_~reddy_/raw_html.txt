His\n\tprimary research interests are <em>Data Mining</em> and <em>Machine Learning</em>\n\twith applications to <em>Healthcare Analytics</em> and <em>Social Network Analysis</em>. His research has been funded by NSF, NIH, DOE, DOT and various industries. He has published over 120 peer-reviewed\n\tarticles in leading conferences and journals. He received several awards for his research work including the <strong><i><span style=\"font-weight:normal;mso-bidi-font-weight:bold\">Best Application Paper\n\tAward</span></i></strong><b style=\"mso-bidi-font-weight:normal\"> </b>at ACM\n\tSIGKDD conference in 2010, <strong><i><span style=\"font-weight:normal;mso-bidi-font-weight:bold\">Best Poster\n\tAward</span></i></strong><b style=\"mso-bidi-font-weight:normal\"> </b>at IEEE VAST conference in 2014, <strong><i><span style=\"font-weight:normal;mso-bidi-font-weight:bold\">Best Student Paper\n\tAward</span></i></strong><b style=\"mso-bidi-font-weight:normal\"> </b>at IEEE ICDM conference in 2016, and was a finalist of the INFORMS Franz Edelman Award Competition in 2011.
