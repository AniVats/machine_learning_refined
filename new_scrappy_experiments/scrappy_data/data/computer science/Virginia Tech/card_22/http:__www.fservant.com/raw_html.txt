His research focuses on <code>software development productivity</code> and <code>software quality</code>.</p>\n\n<p>Francisco uses software evolution analysis and program analysis to create practical, efficient, and human-friendly techniques and tools that provide automatic support for all stages of software development. His research interests include software development productivity, software quality, mining of software repositories, program comprehension, and software visualization.\nHe has published articles in these areas at top software engineering conferences (e.g., ICSE, FSE, ASE) and he has performed research for large technology companies, such as Microsoft Research and DreamWorks Animation.</p>\n\n<p>Francisco received a Ph.D. in Software Engineering from the <a href=\"http://www.uci.edu\">University of California, Irvine</a>, under the supervision of <a href=\"http://www.jamesajones.com\">James A. Jones</a>. He also holds a M.S. in Information and Computer Sciences from the same university supervised by <a href=\"http://www.ics.uci.edu/~andre/\">Andr\u00e9 van der Hoek</a>.
