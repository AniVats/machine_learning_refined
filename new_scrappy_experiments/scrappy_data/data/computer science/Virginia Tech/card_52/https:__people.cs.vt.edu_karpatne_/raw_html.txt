My research builds the foundation of this paradigm and I am currently exploring this paradigm for problems in diverse disciplines such as hydrology, climate science, and computational chemistry. <br><a href=\"#tgds_tkde2017\" class=\"button\" role=\"button\">TKDE 2017</a>\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t<a href=\"#sdm2018\" class=\"button\" role=\"button\">ArXiv 2017</a></p>\n\t\t\t</div>\n\t\t\t<div class=\"resume-content mr-auto\">\n\t\t\t<span class=\"d-none d-lg-block\">\n          <img class=\"img-fluid mx-auto mb-2\" src=\"images/tgds.png\" alt=\"\">\n        </span>  \n\t\t\t</div>\n\t\t\t</div>\n\t\t\t\n\t\t\t<h5>Introduction to Data Mining (Second Edition)</h5>\n\t\t<div class=\"resume-item d-flex flex-column flex-md-row mb-5\">\n\t\t<div class=\"resume-content mr-auto\">  \n        \t<p>The <a href=\"https://www-users.cs.umn.edu/~kumar001/dmbook/index.php\" target=\"_blank\">second edition of this textbook</a> presents new and improved content on several essential topics in data mining such as model overfitting, model evaluation, deep learning, class imbalance, and anomaly detection. Additionally, it introduces an entirely new chapter on <i>avoiding false discoveries</i> for data mining problems\u2013a contribution missing in alternate resources at the required depth and breadth despite its importance. <br><a href=\"#dmbook2017\" class=\"button\" role=\"button\">Book2017</a></p>\n\t\t\t</div>\n\t\t\t<div class=\"resume-content mr-auto\">\n\t\t\t<span class=\"d-none d-lg-block\">\n          <img class=\"img-fluid mx-auto mb-2\" src=\"images/dmbook2e_cover.png\" alt=\"\">\n        </span>  \n\t\t\t</div>\n\t\t\t</div>\n\t\t\t\n\t\t\t<h5>Spatio-temporal Data Mining</h5>\n\t\t<div class=\"resume-item d-flex flex-column flex-md-row mb-5\">\n\t\t<div class=\"resume-content mr-auto\">  \n        \t<p>Space and time introduce several challenges and opportunities for classical data mining algorithms given the variety of data types, representations, problems, and methods in spatio-temporal settings. My recent survey provides an over-arching structure to the vast and diverse field of spatio-temporal data mining. A recurring theme of my research is to equip data mining methods with a better ability to deal with spatio-temporal data from Earth and environmental sciences. <br><a href=\"#csur2017\" class=\"button\" role=\"button\">CSUR2017</a>\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t<a href=\"#kdd2017\" class=\"button\" role=\"button\">KDD2017</a>\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t<a href=\"#bigdata2017\" class=\"button\" role=\"button\">BigData2017</a>\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t<a href=\"#bigdata2016\" class=\"button\" role=\"button\">BigData2016</a>\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t<a href=\"#grsm2016\" class=\"button\" role=\"button\">GRSM2016</a>\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t<a href=\"#cise2015\" class=\"button\" role=\"button\">CISE2015</a>\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t<a href=\"#isgeo_tkde2017\" class=\"button\" role=\"button\">ArXiv 2017</a></p>\n\t\t\t</div>\n\t\t\t<div class=\"resume-content mr-auto\">\n\t\t\t<span class=\"d-none d-lg-block\">\n          <img class=\"img-fluid mx-auto mb-2\" src=\"images/stdm.gif\" alt=\"\">\n        </span>  \n\t\t\t</div>\n\t\t\t</div>\n\t\t\n\t\t<h5>Predictive Learning with Heterogeneity in Populations</h5>\n\t\t<div class=\"resume-item d-flex flex-column flex-md-row mb-5\">\n\t\t<div class=\"resume-content mr-auto\">  \n        \t<p>A central challenge in applying standard predictive learning methods for real-world problems is the heterogeneity in data populations, i.e., different groups of instances show different nature of predictive relationships. My dissertation research introduced several novel ways for addressing this challenge, building on ideas from <i>multi-task learning</i> and <i>group-specific local learning</i>.<br>\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t<a href=\"#thesis2017\" class=\"button\" role=\"button\">Thesis2017</a>\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t<a href=\"#icdm2015\" class=\"button\" role=\"button\">ICDM2015</a>\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t<a href=\"#sdm2015\" class=\"button\" role=\"button\">SDM2015</a>\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t<a href=\"#sdm2014\" class=\"button\" role=\"button\">SDM2014</a>\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t<a href=\"#cidu2012importance\" class=\"button\" role=\"button\">CIDU2012</a></p>\n\t\t\t</div>\n\t\t\t<div class=\"resume-content mr-auto\">\n\t\t\t<span class=\"d-none d-lg-block\">\n          <img class=\"img-fluid mx-auto mb-2\" src=\"images/het.png\" alt=\"\">\n        </span>  \n\t\t\t</div>\n\t\t\t</div>\n\t\t\t\n\t\t\t<h5>Global System for Mapping Surface Water Dynamics</h5>\n\t\t<div class=\"resume-item d-flex flex-column flex-md-row mb-5\">\n\t\t<div class=\"resume-content mr-auto\">  \n        \t<p>My research has enabled a <a href=\"http://z.umn.edu/monitoringwater\" target=\"_blank\">global surface water monitoring system</a> that provides the <i>first global history of surface water</i> every 8 days for the last 15 years using high-resolution satellite data. This system captures vital information about changes occurring in surface water such as droughts, dam constructions, river meandering, and melting glacial lakes, which was featured in an <a href=\"https://nsf.gov/discoveries/disc_summ.jsp?cntn_id=189519\" target=\"_blank\">NSF news story</a>.<br>\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t<a href=\"#rse2017\" class=\"button\" role=\"button\">RSE2017</a>\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t<a href=\"#compsus2016\" class=\"button\" role=\"button\">CompSus2016</a>\n\t\t\t\t\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t  </p><div id=\"water_collapse\" class=\"panel-collapse collapse\"><p></p>\n\t\t\t</div>\n\t\t\t<div class=\"resume-content mr-auto\">\n\t\t\t<span class=\"d-none d-lg-block\">\n          <img class=\"img-fluid mx-auto mb-2\" src=\"images/water.gif\" alt=\"\">\n        </span>  \n\t\t\t</div>\n\t\t\t</div>\n\t\t\t\n      </div></section>\n\n      <hr class=\"m-0\">\n\n      <section class=\"resume-section p-3 p-lg-5 d-flex flex-column\" id=\"publications\">\n        <h3>Publications</h3>\n\t\t  \t<br>\n        \n        \t\t\t\t\t<div class=\"container-fluid\" align=\"left\">\n<h4>Book</h4>\n\n<p>\n\n<b>[B1]</b> P.
