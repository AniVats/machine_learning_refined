In this work, she enjoys collaborating with colleagues in a wide variety of fields, from architectural design and neuropsychology to engineering and medicine.<br>\n<br>\nDr. Interrante is a recipient of the 1999 <a href=\"http://www.nsf.gov/od/lpa/news/press/00/pr0022.htm\" ,=\"\" target=\"_newwindow\">Presidential Early Career Award for Scientists and Engineers</a>, \"the highest honor bestowed by the U.S. government on outstanding scientists and engineers beginning their independent careers\", and a 2001-2003 <a href=\"http://www.scholarswalk.umn.edu/awards/mcknight/mcknight_landgrant.html\" ,=\"\" target=\"_newwindow\">McKnight Land-Grant Professorship</a> from the University of Minnesota.<br>\n<br>\nShe co-founded and served as the first general co-chair of the <a href=\"http://sap.acm.org/\" ,=\"\" target=\"_newwindow\">ACM/SIGGRAPH Symposium on Applied Perception</a>, and is currently serving as co-editor-in-chief of the <a href=\"http://tap.acm.org\" ,=\"\" target=\"_newwindow\">ACM Transactions on Applied Perception</a>, as well as engaging in recent or current service on the editorial boards of several other leading journals including the <a href=\"https://www.computer.org/web/tvcg\" ,=\"\" target=\"_newwindow\">IEEE Transactions on Visualization and Computer Graphics</a>. She was an invited presenter at the 2004 <a href=\"https://www.nap.edu/read/11153/chapter/15#227\" ,=\"\" target=\"_newwindow\">National Academies Convocation on Facilitating Interdisciplinary Research</a>, an <a href=\"https://www.naefrontiers.org/18985/Victoria-Interrante\" ,=\"\" target=\"_newwindow\">invited participant at the National Academy of Engineering's Seventh Annual Frontiers of Engineering</a>, and a member of the organizing committee for the <a href=\"https://www.humboldt-foundation.de/web/944850.html\" ,=\"\" target=\"_newwindow\"> Eighth German-American Symposium on Frontiers of Engineering</a>, a joint project of the NAE and the Alexander von Humbolt Foundation.<br>\n<br>\nIn addition, Dr. Interrante has an active record of leadership and service with numerous top international conferences in virtual reality, visualization and computer graphics.  She is currently a member of the Steering Committee for the <a href=\"http://ieeevr.org/\" ,=\"\" target=\"_newwindow\">IEEE Virtual Reality Conference</a>, for which she also served as <a href=\"http://ieeevr.org/2014/\" ,=\"\" target=\"_newwindow\">co-General Chair in 2014</a> and co-Program Chair in 2015-2017. In recent years, she has also served as the co-General Chair of <a href=\"https://www.frontiersin.org/research-topics/6707/best-papers-from-eurovr-2017\" ,=\"\" target=\"_newwindow\">EuroVR 2017</a>, and as co-Program Chair of the <a href=\"https://www.springer.com/gp/book/9783030017897\" ,=\"\" target=\"_newwindow\">2018</a> and <a href=\"https://eurovr2019.eu\" ,=\"\" target=\"_newwindow\">2019</a> EuroVR Conferences, the <a href=\"https://www.interaction-design.org/literature/conference/proceedings-of-the-joint-virtual-reality-conference-of-egve-eurovr-vec\" ,=\"\" target=\"_newwindow\">2010 Joint Virtual Reality Conference of EGVE, EuroVR and VEC</a>, and the <a href=\"https://diglib.eg.org/handle/10.2312/319\" ,=\"\" target=\"_newwindow\">2008 Eurographics Workshop on Computational Aesthetics</a>, as well as chair of the technical track on Graphics, Animation and Gaming at the 2015 <a href=\"http://gracehopper.org\" ,=\"\" target=\"_newwindow\">Grace Hopper Celebration of Women in Computing</a>.<br>\n<br>\nAt the University of Minnesota, Dr. Interrante is currently serving as the director of the <a href=\"http://www.cogsci.umn.edu\" ,=\"\" target=\"_newwindow\">Center for Cognitive Sciences</a> and as a member of the graduate faculty of the <a href=\"http://humanfactors.design.umn.edu\" ,=\"\" target=\"_newwindow\">Program in Human Factors</a>.
