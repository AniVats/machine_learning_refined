<h3><a name=\"research\">Research</a></h3>\n    <p> <span style=\"color: rgb(0, 50, 255);\"> Firs of all, I am always\n        looking for <i> self-motivated, creative and hard-working </i>\n        graduate and undergradtaue students (and also post-docs, when\n        funding is available) to work with us on a number of research\n        projects. If you are interested, please email me with the <i><b>Subject</b></i>\n        line: <span style=\"color: rgb(255, 0, 0);\"> <b>\"Interested in\n            Ph.D (or MS, or undergraduate, or Post-Doc) Research\n            Opportunities\"</b></span>, together with a short description\n        of your background and research interests/experiences and a CV.</span>\n    </p>\n    Now a bit more about my research: My research interests lie broadly\n    in computer communication and networks, Internet technology,\n    multimedia and emerging applications. While my past research was\n    centered on the analysis, design and development of scalable\n    Internet QoS solutions to support performance-demanding multimedia\n    applications, my current research thrusts focus primarily on i)\n    building <i> highly scalable, resilient and secure</i> Internet\n    infrastructure and mechanisms to enhance Internet service\n    performance, availability, reliability, manageability and security;\n    and on ii) developing <i> next-generation, service-oriented,\n      secure, manageable and economically viable </i> Internet\n    architectures to provide better support for creation, deployment,\n    operations and management of value-added Internet services (e.g.,\n    large-scale mobile, cloud services and content (esp. video) delivery\n    systems) and underlying networks.
