enabled quantitative measurement of diverse cellular phenomena.\n            For instance, microarray technology allows biologists to measure the expression of all genes in the genome\n            on Other technology allows high-throughput measurement of physical interactions between\n            proteins, which are an important mechanism behind most cellular processes. These recent developments have\n            generated an unprecedented amount of data for several different organisms. These data promise to\n            revolutionize our understanding of biology, but integrating information across several noisy, heterogeneous\n            datasets to derive holistic models of the cell requires sophisticated computational approaches.\n\n            Our research focuses on machine learning approaches for integrating diverse genomic data to make inferences\n            about biological networks.
