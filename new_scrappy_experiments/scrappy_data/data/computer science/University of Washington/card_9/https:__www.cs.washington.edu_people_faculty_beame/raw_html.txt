A major focus of his research is in proving lower\nbounds on the resources needed for solving computational problems. Such topics include <a href=\"/people/faculty/beame/publications-by-topic#beame-commstream\">communication complexity</a>, \n<a href=\"/people/faculty/beame/publications-by-topic#beame-tradeoff\">time-space tradeoff lower bounds</a>, \n<a href=\"/people/faculty/beame/publications-by-topic#beame-circuit\">circuit complexity</a>, \n<a href=\"/people/faculty/beame/publications-by-topic#beame-proofsat\">proof complexity</a>,\nand <a href=\"/people/faculty/beame/publications-by-topic#beame-data\">data structures</a>.\nAnother focus of his research is on <a href=\"/people/faculty/beame/publications-by-topic#beame-proofsat\">problems related \nto formal reasoning, including SAT-solving</a>.
