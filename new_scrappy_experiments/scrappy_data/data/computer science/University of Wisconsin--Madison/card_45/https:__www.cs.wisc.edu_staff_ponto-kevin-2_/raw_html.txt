Virtual Reality, Ubiquitous Computing, Human Computer Interaction and Design. &nbsp;I have active collaborations with departments across campus, such as Art, Computer Science, Communication Arts, Engineering (Bio-medical, Industrial, Mechanical), Nursing, and Theater. &nbsp;My teaching focuses on subjects of wearable computing and technology along with virtual and augmented reality.
