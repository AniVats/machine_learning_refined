It turns out there are many such functions beyond parity; these functions are known in cryptography as the correlation immune functions, and they arise within nature in areas such as genetics and protein-protein interactions.  The traditional ML approach to learning such functions is lookahead, but lookahead has high costs both in runtime and susceptibility to overfitting.  Skewing is an alternative approach to learning correlation immune functions.  \n\n<p>\n\n</p></a><ul><a name=\"DT\">\n</a><li><a name=\"DT\"> Lantz, E., Ray, S. and Page, D.
