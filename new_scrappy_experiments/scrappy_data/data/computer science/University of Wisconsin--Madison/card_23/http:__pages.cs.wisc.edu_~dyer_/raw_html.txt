Dyer\" border=\"0\" align=\"CENTER\"> </td>\n</tr>\n</tbody></table>\n<b>Research Interests:</b><br>\nComputer vision, image analysis\n</p><p>\n</p><hr size=\"3\">\n<p>\n\n<!-- BEGIN the body of the web page here -->\n\n</p><p>\n<b>Ph.D.</b>, Computer Science, University of Maryland, 1979\n(<a href=\"dyer-cv.pdf\">Curriculum Vitae</a>)\n\n</p><p>\n<b>Biography:</b><br>\n</p><p>\n\nCharles Dyer is Emeritus Professor of Computer Sciences and \nBiostatistics and Medical Informatics at the University of\nWisconsin - Madison, where he has been on the faculty since 1982.\nHe served as Chair of the Department of Computer Sciences from 1990 to 1993,\nand as Associate Chair from 1987 to 1990.\nHe received his Ph.D. degree in computer science from the University of\nMaryland in 1979, his M.S. degree in computer science from UCLA in 1974,\nand his B.S. degree in mathematical sciences from Stanford University in 1973.  \nHe has worked in the field of computer\nvision for over 35 years and has published over 150 technical\npapers.  \nHis research has spanned a broad range of topics in computer vision and image analysis including\n2D and 3D shape and image representations such as\nquadtrees, aspect graphs, and pyramid algorithms; 3D scene reconstruction;\ncomputational photography;\nobject and face recognition; texture analysis; and image segmentation.  \n</p><p>\n\nDr. Dyer is a Fellow of the Institute of Electrical and Electronics\nEngineers (IEEE) and a Fellow of the International Association\nfor Pattern Recognition (IAPR).\nHe was awarded the\nVisiting Fellowship at Exeter College, University of Oxford\nfor 1999-2000.  He was an associate editor of the journals\n<i>Machine Vision and Applications</i>\nand\n<i>IEEE Transactions on Pattern Analysis and\nMachine Intelligence</i>.  He was General Co-Chair of the\n2003 Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition Conference,\nand Program Co-Chair\nof this same conference in 1996.\nFinally, he is a member of the \n<a href=\"http://www.cs.umd.edu/alumni/\">Alumni Hall of Fame</a> in the Department\nof Computer Science at the University of Maryland.  \n</p><p>\n\n<!-- END of body of the web page -->\n <!-- ************End of Editable Region for Page Content ************************* -->\n  </p>\n  </td>\n  </tr>\n</tbody></table>\n</td>\n\n<td width=\"1%\"><img src=\"http://www.cs.wisc.edu/images/trans.gif\" width=\"20\" height=\"1\" alt=\"\"></td>\n\n</tr>\n\n<tr valign=\"middle\">\n<td width=\"1%\" class=\"bgred\" height=\"20\">&nbsp;</td>\n<td width=\"1%\" valign=\"top\" class=\"bg-vert-shadow\"><img src=\"http://www.cs.wisc.edu/images/trans.gif\" width=\"20\" height=\"1\" alt=\"\"></td>\n<td width=\"97%\" valign=\"top\"><img src=\"http://www.cs.wisc.edu/images/trans.gif\" width=\"20\" height=\"1\" alt=\"\"></td>\n<td width=\"1%\" valign=\"top\"><img src=\"http://www.cs.wisc.edu/images/trans.gif\" width=\"20\" height=\"1\" alt=\"\"></td>\n</tr>\n<tr valign=\"middle\">\n    <td colspan=\"4\" align=\"center\" class=\"bgblack\" height=\"20\"> <font size=\"1\"><span class=\"bodywhite\">\n\t\n<!-- **************** Start of Editable Region for bottom navigation bar *********************** -->\n\n\t<a href=\"http://www.cs.wisc.edu \">Department of Computer Sciences</a> \n\t<font color=\"white\">|</font> \n\t<a href=\"http://www.wisc.edu\">University of Wisconsin -
