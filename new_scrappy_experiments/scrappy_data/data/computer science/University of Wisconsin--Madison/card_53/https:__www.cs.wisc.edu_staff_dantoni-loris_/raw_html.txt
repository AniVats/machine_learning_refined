    <div class=\"col-md-6\">\n                            <h4>Foundations of program synthesis</h4>\n                            <p> Lay the foundations of program synthesis.\n                            Can we predict when and understand why synthesis works?\n                            Can we solve synthesis problems with quantitative objectives?\n                            What is the relation between synthesis and machine learning?\n                            Read more <a href=\"https://nsf.gov/awardsearch/showAward?AWD_ID=1750965&amp;HistoricalAwards=false\">here</a>.\n                            </p>\n                        </div>\n                        <div class=\"col-md-6\">\n                            <h4>Verifiable machine learning</h4>\n                            <p>\n                            Design scalable and effective formal methods for proving\n                            robustness, fairness, and other properties of machine learning algorithms and models.\n                            Take a look at <a href=\"https://arxiv.org/abs/1912.00981\">Antidote</a> and <a href=\"papers/oopsla17.pdf\">FairSquare</a>.</p>\n                        </div>\n                    </div>\n                    <div class=\"row\">\n                        \n                        \n                        <div class=\"col-md-6\">\n                            <h4>Intent-based networking</h4>\n                            <p>Allow network operators to verify, design, and repair network configurations by providing high-level\n                            intents (i.e., desirable network policies) instead of low-level configurations that are hard to program.\n                            Take a look at <a href=\"papers/popl17genesis.pdf\">Genesis</a> and <a href=\"https://arxiv.org/abs/1912.02402\">D2R</a>.\n                            </p>\n                            \n                        </div>\n                        <div class=\"col-md-6\">\n solvers) to reason\n                            about strings and tress over complex and infinite alphabets.\n                            Read more <a href=\"symbolicautomata.html\">here</a>.</p>\n
