I care about how computing and information technologies can serve people untethered, or <em>Mobile Computing</em>. In this space, my students and I have built <a href=\"http://www.k2os.org/\">mobile operating systems</a>, <a href=\"https://www.yecl.org/publications/likamwa2016isca.pdf\">mixed-signal sensors</a>, <a href=\"http://argos.rice.edu/\">world's first massive MIMO base stations</a>, as well as <a href=\"https://www.recg.org/rio.html\">novel ways for mobile systems to collaborate</a> and <a href=\"http://livelab.recg.rice.edu/\">the first publicly reported longitudinal study of smartphone users in the field</a>. Lately I am interested in pushing the limits of software systems by embracing analog hardware, formal methods, and new systems programming languages. We are building <a href=\"https://www.yecl.org/publications/boos2017plos.pdf\">a new operating system</a> with baremetal efficiency and runtime composability for edge servers and embedded systems alike.</p>\n\n<p>I am currently on leave with Microsoft Research (Redmond) as a visiting researcher.</p>\n\n    <hr>\n\n     <p>If you are interested in using our Argos massive MIMO systems, they are commericially available from <a href=\"http://www.skylarkwireless.com/\">Skylark Wireless</a>, a starup co-founded by my former Ph.D. student Clay Shepard.\n \tThey will also be available soon as part of <a href=\"https://www.nsf.gov/awardsearch/showAward?AWD_ID=1827940\">the NSF-funded <em>Platform for Advanced Wireless Research</em> (PAWR) program</a>.</p>\n\n<hr>\n<p style=\"text-align:center\"><a href=\"./bio.txt\">Biosketch</a>, <a href=\"./cv_zhong.pdf\">Full CV</a></p>\n\n<hr>\n\n<p>If you enjoy buidling systems and find our recent work interesting, email me with your CV for opportunities.</p>\t\n\n<hr>\n<p><i><b>Ph.D. Alumni</b></i></p>\n                        <ul>\n\n                          <li><a href=\"http://mhyun.web.rice.edu/\">Min Hong Yun</a> (Google): Ph.D., 2018 (Distinguished Paper Finalist, NDSS'19).</li> \n                      \n\n<li> <a href=\"http://clay.rice.edu/\">Clayton\n                              Shepard</a> (CTO and Co-founder of Skylark Wireless): Ph.D., 2017.</li>\n                          \n\n<li> <a href=\"http://www.roblkw.com/\">Robert\n                              LiKamWa</a> (Assistant Professor, Arizona State EE &amp; Media): Ph.D., 2016.
