property=\"content:encoded\"><h2>Website&nbsp;Accessibility</h2>\n<p>As part of <a href=\"https://www.yale.edu/about-yale/mission-statement\" class=\"ext\">Yale\u2019s mission<span class=\"ext\"><span class=\"element-invisible\"> (link is external)</span></span></a>, Yale is committed to \u201cthe free exchange of ideas in an ethical, interdependent, and diverse community.\u201d That diversity includes all people regardless of ability or disability.</p>\n<p>Websites and web applications are integral to the university\u2019s academic and administrative work. Yale is committed to making information, programs and activities on its websites and web applications accessible to people with disabilities through its&nbsp;<a href=\"https://your.yale.edu/policies-procedures/policies/1605-web-accessibility-policy\" class=\"ext\">Web Accessibility Policy<span class=\"ext\"><span class=\"element-invisible\"> (link is external)</span></span></a>.</p>\n<p>The Web Accessibility Policy&nbsp;applies to websites and applications that conduct university business. Owners of such sites should be familiar with this policy&nbsp;and the <a href=\"https://your.yale.edu/policies-procedures/procedures/1605-pr01-web-accessibility-procedure\" class=\"ext\">Web Accessibility Procedure<span class=\"ext\"><span class=\"element-invisible\"> (link is external)</span></span></a>. The policy may also affect people in roles that work on university websites. People in roles that work on University websites and web applications should explore this website and take advantage of <a href=\"/web-accessibility/articles/learning-accessibility\">Yale\u2019s accessibility best practices</a>&nbsp;and <a href=\"/web-accessibility/training\">trainings</a>.</p>\n<p>To meet the requirements of the policy, university websites should comply with the <a href=\"/web-accessibility/articles/wcag-overview\">Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (\u201cWCAG\u201d) 2 Level AA</a>. To see answers to common questions about accessibility, including how the policy may affect you, visit the <a href=\"/web-accessibility/accessibility-yale/accessibility-faqs\">Frequently Asked Questions</a> page.</p>\n<p>Common questions about accessibility at Yale are answered on the&nbsp;<a href=\"/web-accessibility/accessibility-yale/accessibility-faqs\">Frequently Asked Questions</a>&nbsp;page. Email other questions to <a href=\"mailto:accessibility@yale.edu\" class=\"mailto\">accessibility@yale.edu<span class=\"mailto\"><span class=\"element-invisible\"> (link sends e-mail)</span></span></a></p>\n<h2>Other Yale Accessibility&nbsp;Resources</h2>\n<ul><li><a href=\"https://sas.yale.edu\" class=\"ext\">Student Accessibility Services<span class=\"ext\"><span
