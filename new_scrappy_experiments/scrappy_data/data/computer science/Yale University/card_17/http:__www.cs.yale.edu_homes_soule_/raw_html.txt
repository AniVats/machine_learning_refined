id=\"biography\">Biography</h2>\n\n\n<p>\n  I am an Assistant Professor at <a href=\"https://www.yale.edu\">Yale\nUniversity</a> and a Research Scientist at <a href=\"https://barefootnetworks.com\">Barefoot\n    Networks</a>, an Intel Company.\n  Prior to joining Yale, I was an\nAssociate Professor at\nthe <a href=\"https://www.inf.usi.ch/en\">Universit\u00e0 della\nSvizzera italiana</a> in Lugano, Switzerland, where I still hold an\nAdjunct Professor position.  I received my B.A. from Brown University,\nand my Ph.D. from NYU. After my Ph.D., I was a post-doc at Cornell\nUniversity. Concurrent with my graduate studies, I was a research\nco-op in the Data Intensive Systems and Analytics Group at IBM\nT. J. Watson Research Center for two years.\n</p>\n\n\n</section>\n\n\n<!-- ================================================================== -->\n<section>\n<h2 id=\"group\">Research Group</h2>\n\n<h3>PhD
