class=\"col-sm-12\">\n        <h1>CMU/CU Boulder Engineering Partnership Programs</h1>\n    </div>\n</div>\n        \n                    \n                                                 <div class=\"row\">\n                        <div class=\"col-sm-12\">\n                            <p>Colorado Mesa University and the University of Colorado Boulder have created three innovative partnerships to deliver civil, mechanical, and electrical and computer engineering programs in their entirety in Grand Junction. The first two years of the programs are taught by CMU faculty and the second two years of the programs are taught by CU Boulder faculty who live permanently in Grand Junction.</p>\n<p>Students completing either the Civil, Mechanical Engineering, or Electrical and Computer Engineering Partnership Program will receive the commensurate Bachelor of Science degree in either Civil, Mechanical, or Electrical and Computer Engineering (BSCE, BSME, BSECE*) from the University of Colorado Boulder.&nbsp;</p>\n<p>The Civil, Mechanical Engineering, and Electrical and Computer Partnership Programs prepare students for careers in a wide range of industries through the rigorous study of either civil, mechanical, or electrical and computer engineering. Students participate in hands-on engineering design projects beginning in their freshman year and continuing through senior design projects.</p>\n<p>Residence at CMU provides CU Boulder engineering students the unique option of small class size and more contact with faculty while experiencing an engineering program equal in quality to that delivered on the CU Boulder campus.</p>\n<p>*Degrees awarded by University of Colorado Boulder</p>\n<p><a href=\"finances-partnership-student-matrix.pdf\">Financial/Fees Matrix for CU/CMU Partnership Students</a></p>\n                        </div>\n
