He received his Ph.D. from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology. <b>RESEARCH:</b> His main research areas include Computer Vision, Artificial Intelligence, and Machine Learning with a focus on image/video analysis and automated surveillance. He has received over $3M in research funding (to date) from the National Science Foundation (including a prestigious CAREER award), U.S. Air Force, Los Alamos National Lab, U.S. Army Night Vision Laboratory, Intel, and Ohio Board of Regents. \n<b>TEACHING:</b> Prof. Davis offers classes in Computer Vision (CSE 5524) and Artificial Intelligence (CSE 5052) at Ohio State University. The Artificial Intelligence course was specifically designed to fill a need and outreach to the large body of non-CSE students interested in the topic.\n<b>SERVICE:</b> In addition to his Full
