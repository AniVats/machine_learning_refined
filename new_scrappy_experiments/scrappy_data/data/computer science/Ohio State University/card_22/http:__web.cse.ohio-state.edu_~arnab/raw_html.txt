id=\"p3\">&nbsp;</span><span id=\"p4\">&nbsp;</span>\n<p> Arnab\u2019s research focuses on user-facing challenges in large-scale data analytics and interactive data exploration. The goal of <a href=\"http://interact.osu.edu\">his group</a> is to <strong>empower humans to effectively interact with data</strong>. This involves solving problems that span the areas of database systems, visualization, human-computer interaction, and information retrieval.</p>\n<p></p>\n<p>Arnab serves on the steering committee of the annual Workshop on Human-in-the-Loop Data Analytics (<a href=\"http://hilda.io\"><span class=\"caps\">HILDA</span></a>). Arnab is also a founder of <a href=\"http://steamfactory.osu.edu\">The <span class=\"caps\">STEAM</span> Factory</a>, a collaborative interdisciplinary research and public outreach initiative, and the <a href=\"http://arnab.org/about/ohio-ohio-states-hackathon-program\"><span class=\"caps\">OHI</span>/O Hackathon Program</a>, that uses hackathons as an informal learning platform. Arnab is a recipient of the <a href=\"http://www.nsf.gov/awardsearch/showAward?AWD_ID=1453582&amp;HistoricalAwards=false\"><span class=\"caps\">NSF</span> <span class=\"caps\">CAREER</span> Award</a>, a <a href=\"http://googleresearch.blogspot.com/2012/07/google-research-awards-summer-2012.html\">Google Faculty Research Award</a>, and the Ohio State College of Engineering <a href=\"https://engineering.osu.edu/news/2016/05/honoring-excellence-19th-annual-distinguished-faculty-awards\">Lumley Research Award</a>. He is also the recipient of the <span class=\"caps\">IEEE</span> <span class=\"caps\">TCDE</span> <a href=\"http://tab.computer.org/tcde/tcdeawardsrecipients.html\">Early Career Award</a> for his contributions towards user-focused data interaction.
