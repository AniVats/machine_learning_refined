Informatics (BMI), and the Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE). He leads the AIMed (Artificial Intelligence in Medicine) Lab at OSU. Before joining OSU in January 2019, he was a researcher at IBM TJ Watson Research Center. His research focuses on <font size=\"+1.00\"><b>data mining</b></font>, <font size=\"+1.00\"><b>machine learning</b></font> and their applications to <font size=\"+1.00\"><b>biomedical informatics</b></font>.</p>\n<h2>Openings</h2>\n<p><a href=\"https://web.cse.ohio-state.edu/~zhang.10631/lab.html\">AIMed Lab</a> currently has openings for <a href=\"https://web.cse.ohio-state.edu/~zhang.10631/files/ad_phd.txt\">PhD students (fall 2020)</a>, <a href=\"https://web.cse.ohio-state.edu/~zhang.10631/files/ad_postdoc.txt\">postdoctoral associates</a>, and visiting scholars with research interests related to health data science and/or AI algorithms in medicine.
